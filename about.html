About Me

Get to know me beyond the code

Professor Nate International

Hello! I'm Nathan Musonda (GitHub: Shock-father), known professionally as "Professor Nate". I'm a Zambian Computer Science student and software developer with a passion for creating practical technology solutions that make a difference.

I believe in building technology that solves real problems for real people, especially in the Zambian context.

Educational Journey

My educational journey began at North Park Private School, followed by boarding school at Moomba Secondary School where I developed discipline and leadership skills. I hold professional certifications including a Klagg Certificate and Huawei Certification in networking and telecommunications.

Professor Nate International

Through my education initiative, I focus on bridging the gap between academic knowledge and practical software development. I mentor aspiring developers and create resources that help others build real-world programming skills. My goal is to empower the next generation of Zambian tech talent.

Current Focus

I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science while working on full-stack projects, mobile applications, and AI integrations. I'm particularly interested in creating solutions for education, logistics, and business needs in Zambia.

Technology Stack

Python is my primary language, complemented by Flask/Django for web development, Flutter for mobile apps, MySQL/PostgreSQL for databases, and TensorFlow for AI/ML implementations. I'm always exploring new technologies to expand my toolkit.
